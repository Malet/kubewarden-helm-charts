kubewarden-controller installed.

You can start defining cluster admission policies by using the
`clusteradmissionpolicies.policies.kubewarden.io` resource.

For more information checkout https://kubewarden.io/

> **Warning:** the `ClusterAdmissionPolicy` resources of version `v1alpha1` are
> now deprecated. The next release of `kubewarden-controller` won't support
> them anymore.
>
> This release of kubewarden-controller is not reconciling `v1alpha1` resources
> anymore.
>
> Migrate your `ClusterAdmissionPolicy` to `v1alpha2`.
